      RoamBot - Hack Lassonde

      Roambot was a project I completed for Local Hack Day at York University in 8 hours. The robot was controlled by a person's movement which were detected by an Xbox Kinect. The Kinect attached to my laptop which identified the gesture using the C# Kinect Lib. The computer sent the robot's command over WiFi via UDP to an Android phone that was sitting on the robot. The Android then sent the same instruction over Bluetooth to the Arduino which controlled the motors to drive. Simultaneously, the Android used a 3rd party app to stream video from the back camera to show the front of the robot. This allowed me to control the robot from anywhere within the building where the WiFi reached. I managed to get 1st Place and worked independently.
